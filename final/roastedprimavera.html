Roasted Primavera

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cherry Tomato halves
  • 2 medium Squash diced, your preference
  • 1 Green Pepper sliced
  • 1 Red Onion, chunked
  • 8 ounces of sliced Mushrooms
  • 1 cup Spinach (or your favorite leafy green)
  • ¼ teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • ¼ teaspoon Salt
  • ¼ teaspoon Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon Nutritional Yeast
  • 8 ounces Pasta (your favorite kind)
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • 1 tablespoon Hemp Hearts

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  3. Place all vegetables on a sheet pan. Toss with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, and Italian seasoning. Cook in preheated oven for ~20 min.
  4. Add pasta to boiling water and cook to package directions. Reserve 1 cup of water before draining pasta.
  5. Drain pasta. Return to pot. Add Spinach and stir to wilt.
  6. Add cooked vegetables to pot with nutritional yeast & lemon juice to pot. Stir to combine. Add pasta water to loosen if necessary.
  7. Serve pasta and top with hemp hearts.

Information

This recipe is great with a variety of roasted vegetable to mix and match to your own preference.
Bread crumbs or toasted pine nuts can replace hemp hearts.
Serve with garlic toast or naan and a protein if desired.
