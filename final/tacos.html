Tacos with Avocado Crema

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon EVOO
  • 2 Flour Tortilla Shells
  • 8 ounces corn
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Â¼ cup taco seasoning

Recipe

  1. Dice avocado, 1/2 onion, and 1/2 jalapeno. Add to food processor. Blend with garlic, lemon juice and 1/2 teaspoon of salt until smooth. Add EVOO until desired consistency is reached.
  2. Heat pan over medium high heat with swirl of EVOO.
  3. Dice remaining onion, jalapeno and green pepper. 1/2 cherry tomatoes.
  4. Add onion, jalapeno and green pepper to pan. Cook 3-5 minutes until softened.
  5. Add corn to pan. Mix with other vegetables. Allow to cook without stirring for 3 minutes, until corn begins to blacken.
  6. Stir and add black beans. Heat until warmed.
  7. Add taco seasoning with a splash of water. Mix until distributed.
  8. Serve in shells and top with tomatoes and avocado cream.

Information

Serve with your favorite toppings.
Use in a burrito with vegan cheese and grill for a crispy burrito
Make extra and serve over rice as taco bowls...this is a great leftover lunch option.
