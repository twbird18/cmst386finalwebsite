Sloppy Joe

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2 jalapeno pepper
  • 4 carrots
  • 16 oz mushrooms
  • 16 oz lentils or ground "beef: or both
  • 8 buns

    • Sauce Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup ketchup (or tomato sauce)
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon spicy brown mustard (for a kick use horseradish mustard)
  • 3/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Recipe

  1. Heat pan over medium heat with swirl of oil.
  2. Place vegetables in food processor and chop until fine.
  3. Add all vegetable to pan. Saute for 3 min.
  4. Add beans/beef. Combine with vegetables and cook 5 min.
  5. Add sauce ingredients and stir well to combine cook for 10-15 minutes until sauce has thickened to desired consistency.
  6. Sample and adjust seasoning to taste (i.e. more sugar for a sweeter sauce, more soy for a more savory sauce, more mustard or even a dash of hot sauce for spicier).
  7. Server on buns and enjoy. This recipe will make plenty of leftovers to enjoy for lunch during the week or to freeze for another meal.

Information

Try a variety of vegetables. Cucumber, celery, squashes, etc all make good additions to stretch the mixture.
Add your favorite BBQ sauce in place of Worcestershire & Soy sauce for a BBQ spin on this dish.
This recipe can easily be doubled for future use and can be made in the crockpot by combining all ingredients for ~4 hours on high or 8 hours on low. If cooking in crockpot you may need to adjust sauce by adding more ketchup or water.
For a quicker meal, substitute your favorite canned sloppy joe mix for the sauce.
Top with your favorite toppings cheese, avocado, mayo, coleslaw, pickles, etc.
