Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 8 ounces mushrooms
  • 1 zucchini squash
  • 8 ounces sugar snap peas
  • 1 jalapeno (can be left out for less spice)
  • 8 ounces of pasta
  • 4 servings "meatballs" (I prefer Gardein)
  • 20 ounces crushed roasted tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons salt & pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Recipe

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Heat large pan with olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Dice onion, green pepper, jalapeno pepper, mushrooms & squash.
  4. Add to pan. Add snap peas to pan after removing ends or strings if desired. Add salt, pepper and red pepper. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add garlic, Italian seasoning, tomato sauce and meatballs. Bring to a simmer and cook for the amount of time directed by your chosen meatballs.
  6. Sample sauce. Add seasoning as necessary to your taste.
  7. Add pasta to boiling water and cook according to directions.
  8. Drain pasta and add to sauce.

Information

As a shortcut, a jar of sauce could be used. Vegetables can be substituted as desired.
This recipe can easily be double or tripled to create additional meals. And will freeze and reheat well. Also makes for a great leftover lunch and can be kept in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
Top with Hemp Hearts or bread crumbs or vegan parmesan. Add nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor.
