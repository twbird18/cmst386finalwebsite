Collection of User Information

Vegan4One collects only information that you provide to us. This includes the form located on the contact us page. Any information provided there will be stored and used to contact if requested or deleted once any required actions have been taken if contact is not requested.

Usage of User Information

Vegan4One only gathers information to improve user experience. This includes, but is not limited to: requests for recipe information, future recipe creation requests, site updates, and additional information requests. The information collected helps to keep the site focused and hopefully provide an experience that is useful to site visitors.

Third Party Information Usage

Some advertisements and information provided by Vegan4One may come from third party sources. These companies may collect information about you through the use of our website, for instance, cookie information in order to tailor your advertisements. We do not have access or control over this except to use only relevant third party sources.

Cookie Usage

What are cookies? No, we are not talking about delicious baked good.

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are generally used to make websites work and to provide information to the owners of the site.

How does Vegan4One use cookies?

To optimize the user experience. Cookies help us to tailor your experience, allow you to provide feedback to us, and to communicate with us on other areas of the web.
