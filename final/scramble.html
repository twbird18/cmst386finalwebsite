Tofu Scramble

Ingredients

  • 7 oz extra firm tofu
  • pinch red pepper (to taste)
  • 1-2 tsp oil
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 cup mushrooms
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • water(to thin)

Recipe

  1. Pat tofu dry. Slice. Drain and press.
  2. Heat large skillet over medium heat and add oil.
  3. Prepare vegetable while skillet is heating. Slice onion, pepper, and mushrooms thinly or dice as preferred.
  4. Add to heated pan. Sautee with a pinch of salt & pepper 5-10 min to desired consistency. Add spinach to pan and wilt down 1-2 min.
  5. While spinach is wilting, mix spices together in a bowl and add a splash of water to thin for easy pouring.
  6. Move vegetables to the side and crumble tofu into pan. Sprinkle with salt & pepper. Cook 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add spice mixture to tofu. Cook another 5-8 minutes until tofu is browned and mix together with tofu.
  8. Serve and enjoy!

Information

Serve with skillet potatoes, as a burrito, or with toast. Top with Avocado, Salsa, fresh Tomato, or Hemp Hearts.

Freeze leftovers up to 1 month or refrigerate for up to 5 days.
