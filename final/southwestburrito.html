Southwest Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients

  • 1 potato
  • Tofu Scramble
  • Salsa
  • Avocado
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 Burrito Shells

Recipe

  1. Peel potato while grill, waffle maker, or oven warms up.
  2. Place in food processor and shred to desired consistency.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spoon mixture into waffle maker. Cook to desired crispiness. ~8 minutes in waffle maker. This will take longer in the oven, air fryer, or on a George Foreman Grill.
  5. Warm tofu scramble or make a fresh batch.
  6. Slice Avocado.
  7. Place 1/2 of potato mixture into middle of burrito shell when finished.
  8. Add tofu scramble on top of potatoes.
  9. Add 1/2 sliced avocado.
  10. Top with a couple teaspoons of salsa.
  11. Roll up burrito place in grill or waffle maker until browned.
  12. Repeat steps 6-10 for second burrito.
  13. Enjoy with favorite toppings, more salsa, cheese, sour cream, etc.

Information

This recipe is great as a batch with tofu scramble. Freezes very well for up to a month, if you leave out avocado and salsa. Reheat from frozen ~5 minutes depending on your microwave. Add toppings and enjoy.

Add cheese or sauteed fresh vegetables for more flavor.
