Chocolate Peanut Butter Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup soy milk (or preferred milk)
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon flax seed

Recipe

  1. Heat oats, milk, peanut butter, 1/2 smashed banana and cocoa powder to boil. Turn down and simmer 5 minutes or longer for a thicker oatmeal.
  2. Pour into bowl. Top with sliced strawberries, remaining banana, chocolate chips and flax seed.
  3. Melt tablespoon of peanut butter in microwave for 30 seconds. Drizzle over oatmeal.

Information

Add a teaspoon of sugar, honey, or maple syrup if your banana isn't sweet enough.

Use your preferred toppings, slivered almonds, blueberries, coconut, etc.

Chocolate Milk can be substitud for the milk & cocoa powder.

Works great as overnight oats as well with a little more liquid, just add toppings in the morning and warm if desired.
