Sushi Bowl

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sushi rice (or even leftover rice)
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/4 cup shelled edamame
  • 1 sheet nori
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seed

    • Spicy Mayo

  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha

Recipe

  1. Place rice into bowl.
  2. Heat pan over medium heat.
  3. Cut cucumber into matchsticks. Peel if preferred.
  4. Peel and wash carrot. Then continue using vegetable peeler to create strips of carrot.
  5. Cut avocado into slices.
  6. Add carrot, cucumber, edamame & avocado (see below to use your avocado as decoration) to bowl.
  7. Quickly toast Nori in pre-heated pan. Turning often to prevent burning. Remove when crispy.
  8. Crunch nori sheet over bowl & top with sesame seeds.
  9. Mix Mayo & sriracha together.
  10. Add spicy mayo. (Use only sriracha for a lower calorie meal)

Information

Serve with skillet potatoes, as a burrito, or with toast. Top with Avocado, Salsa, fresh Tomato, or Hemp Hearts.

Freeze leftovers up to 1 month or refrigerate for up to 5 days.
