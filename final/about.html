Vegan4One

Welcome to my food blog! I love to cook quick and easy meals that are healthy and hopefully delicious. I want everyone to be able to enjoy a nice veggie packed meal whenever they feel like it. As a full-time rotating shift worker, I know that you can't always make a meal from scratch so I focus on making nutrious meals for myself that can be created in larger batches for make-ahead lunches or freezer meals. I also try to make meals that can be easily modified for the non-vegan in your life as my partner is not a veggie lover and I'm just not the kind of person who is going to make a completely separate meal for anyone else if I'm cooking.

This site is dedicated to plant-based meals. That means that many of my recipes are vegan, but I do eat some none vegan items such as honey which can easily be substituted if you prefer.
