Vegan 4 One

Food for yourself!

Welcome Everyone!

Here at vegan4one, you'll find quick and easy recipes for feeding one person, often with leftovers for the freezer or a lunch, on a plant-based diet. I work rotating shift work, often with many hours of over-time so I'm a huge believer in cooking once to eat 3 or more times. Most recipes here will either include leftovers for freezing or meal planning or they are easily expanded to create extras if needed.

Freezer Meals

Whether you're new to cooking, budgeting, or simply trying to eat healthier, we welcome everyone. Please post questions or comments. Most recipes can be made with simple substitutions that suite your preferences. For instance, if you don't enjoy ground meat substitute, use your favorite beans or tofu. If you hate a certain vegetable, you can swap it out for your favorite. Looking for a lower calorie meal? Leave out the heavy sauce or cream. Short on time? Choose a meal that can go in the crock pot while you're living your life or substitute fresh vegetable for frozen or canned ones.

Dishes

One recommendation though, as you stock up your freezer with leftovers, make sure you're labeling the dishes with the date & name so you don't forget and so you don't leave anything too long.
